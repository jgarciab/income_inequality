
Title

    WID.world concepts -- List of five-letter codes to use with the wid command.

Content

    Concepts are divided in seven categories:
        1) macro income
        2) distributed income
        3) macro wealth
        4) distributed wealth
        5) wealth/income ratios
        6) population
        7) price and exchange rates
